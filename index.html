Data Security Checklist & How-To Guide for Small Healthcare Providers

Even if your practice is not subject to HIPAA, you still play an important role in protecting your patients' personal and health information. The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) encourages businesses to follow sound data security practices under Section 5 of the FTC Act, which prohibits unfair or deceptive practices. Taking reasonable steps to secure patient data not only reduces risk, but also helps build trust with the people you serve.
